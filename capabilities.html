  • Weldox S690QL
  • Hardox HB400®, HB450®, HB 500®
  • Bars: EN8D, 25MnCr5, 20CrMo4, 20MnV6, 42CrMo4, 42CrMo4+QT

We Follow Customer Standards

WELDING AND FITTING

We redefine precision with 61 welding stations and 61 fitting stations, delivering craftsmanship and quality in every product.

  • Metal Arc Welding as per DTW 1395 2
  • All Welders are Certified as per AWSD1.1
  • AWS D1.1/D1.1M:2015 - Structural Welding Code - Steel
  • Electrodes as per AWS A5.20 & AWS A5.18 (ER70S-3)
  • In-house CWISP 3.1 Welding Inspector approving all procedures
  • AWS D14.3/D.14.3M:2005
